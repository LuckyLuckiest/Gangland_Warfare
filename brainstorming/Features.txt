Features:
	1)	Weapons
	2)	Phone
	3)	Economy
	4)	Police
	5)	Bounty
	6)	Wanted Levels
	7)	Safe
	8)	Dealer
	9)	Gang
	10)	Level system

Weapons:
	Can be created from a text file
	Customizable

Phone:
	Activate bounty on other players
	Activities (Mini missions)
	Teleport - taxi
	Display player info
	Not throwable

Economy:
	Need to link with Vault plugin
	Have custom economy by the plugin

Police:
	Zombies leather armor
	Holds a baton
	Randomly spawn
	Attacks when you have a wanted level

Bounty:
	Bounty amount increases to a set level
	If the time runs out you receive the bounty payment
	If a player kills you they receive double bounty payment

Wanted Level:
	Has 5 stars
	When you have a wanted level of 3+ stars you get a bounty

Safe:
	They are scattered in the world
	Set by admins as safes
	A player can crack the safe using a tool
	They receive money and/or weapons

Dealer:
 	Two types of dealers:
 	1) Acts as black market dealer, that sells forbiden items
 	2) Normal dealer that sells items

Gang:
	A boss, captain, and members
	Their own balance
	Have challenges
	Alliances
	Toggle friendly fire

Level System:
	Have a level
	xp system
